What is Crochet?

Crochet is a textile technique using yarn, thread, or strands interlocked with a crochet hook

How to start to Crocheting?

  • Start from praticing basics, learn different type of stitches
  • Buy cheap yarns to do some trial test before doing advance projects
  • Buy starter kits to pratice on before starting on a more advance piece
  • Looking at different tutorials (choose the ones that you can understand)

There are detailed tutorial videos but for people who are more advance can just refer to patterns sheet

Example of a sling bag pattern sheet (Heart Bag By Breathing Blue)

What can you make with crochet?

  • Keychain, Plushies, Toy(for kids/pets)
  • Bag, Coin Pouch, Wallet, Card Holder
  • Coaster, Cup holder, Tissuebox Cover
  • Clothes: Sweater, Cardigan, Top, Beanie
  • Chair/Sofa Leg Covers(Prevent scratches)
