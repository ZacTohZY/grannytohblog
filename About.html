Who am i?

My name is Zac Toh, and this is not my real name. It is both my nickname and my surname. My blog name gets its name from my family name, and many people believe that those who enjoy these hobbies are granny/old people, which is why my site is called GrannyToh.

Why did i start crocheting?

I began crocheting because I wanted to make a handmade gift for my significant other. When my friends saw what I had made, several of them expressed an interest to learn as well! So I reasoned, "Why not?" Have a place where I can share my knowledge and assist other crocheters. I've been crocheting for a little over two years.

What is crochet to me?

Crochet is a form of art that involves putting stitches together, patching them up, and creating something unique with yarn and a hook. There is no right or wrong, and it does not need to be perfectly stitched.
